3 Commas : FLASH CRASH 04/12/21 Bot Review

"Perfect is the enemy of good."

Good day all! After the recent flash crash on 04 DEC 2021 , I spent some time reflecting on my current botting strategy. Despite being abit overextended , the bots managed to bounce back and I was able to take profit from my deals.

Lets go over the bad. As the whole market crashed , my funds were being used by the bots. Certain bots were not able to average down their buying price and take profit.

Look at the BTC Graph below:

Though I managed to close the deal and take profit soon after. If the deal was closed earlier , funds could be allocated to my other bots. I was fortunate enough to survive through this flash crash. I needed to de-risk.

One thing I am thankful for was implementing 'Deal Start Conditions' to the bots. I was previously using ASAP where the bots will just buy as soon as a deal was completed.

As you can see , my bot bought at the peak and took 21 days to close a deal. If I had place certain trade conditions instead of ASAP , my funds would not be hogged up. Precious time wasted and a part of my capital was being occupied for no reason.

I am thankful that majority of bots stood strong during the flash crash. I need to continue learning and tweaking the settings to ensure I can survive future crashes.
