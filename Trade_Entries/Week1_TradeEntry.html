Week 1 Trading (13-20/10/21)

"Before drawing bases , ask yourself if there are buyers in the market"

Good day all!

Trading is foreign to me. I never thought of trading. With public perception, it seems like you are sacrificing part of your life away for uncertain gains. Long-term buy and hold were the route I took with investments.

I eventually noticed the flaws in the strategy. With limited capital, my profits were limited. As I am serving National Service in Singapore, my monthly allowance is not enough. Relying on injected capital ensures my portfolio remain small.

By holding long, I was dependant on the price only going up. My money was not working during side-ways or the market downtrend. It also assumes my selected coins will survive over a long period of time. No amount of research can protect me from black swan events. Most coins die.

It hit me. Trading helps me de-risk. When taking profit in trading, I will be able to accumulate “free” coins. By not continuously risking my money every time I wanted to buy, I can hold long-term with coins I want without having to chuck more money in.

I watched QuickFingers Luc more actively. Trading was not a short-term get rich quick strategy but a skill that is honed over years. I implemented habits in my morning routine, where I set alerts and draw bases on charts daily. Ensuring that I can wait for the opportunity to arise instead of constantly camping at my phone.

There are no regrets. With charts constantly moving, the next opportunity is always out there. I have to learn that ‘Any profit is a good trade’. It was crucial if I made the right call and execution during trades.
