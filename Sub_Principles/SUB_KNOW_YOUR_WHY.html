1.1)Know what game your are playing.

“I am interested in the game only as I play it for myself and in my own way.” Everyone is born with a unique set of circumstances. You will need to learn to make and trust your own judgement if you expect to achieve anything.

1.2) There is no bull or bear side, but the right side.

“Events, not vanity, proved for me that I could read the tape more accurately than most of the people aroun me.” If your producing the results you desire, is that not good enough. Holding yourself accountable to both your failures and results, allows you to create better systems and though processes.

1.3) Back your opinions. Actually have skin in the game

“When I make money I make it backing my own opinions.” Financial Advisors or Insurance Agents would not touch their own products yet have no issues selling it to others. When listening to advice from others, check if their portfolio reflects what their saying.
