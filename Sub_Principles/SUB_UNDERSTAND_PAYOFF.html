2.1) Know the force behind the thing

a) What are the qualities that lead to a specific outcome? Every achievemnt was the result of being able to repeatedly overcome obstacles. In order to actually succeed, an individual would develop adopt certain kind of mindset. If someone managed to accumulate wealth, it could be due to being frugal or in-different to the lifestyles around them. If someone is fit , they would require to be self-disciplined. If someone is often late, lack of empathy and respect towards other people's time. Adopting a set of characteristics will be revealed in a persons behaviour.

2.2) Do not openly teach others

a)“The average man doesn’t wish to be told that it is a bull or bear market. What he desires is to be told specifically which particular stock to buy or sell. He wants to get something for nothing. He does not wish to work. He doesn’t even wish to have to think. It is too much bother to have to count the money that he picks up from the ground.” When things turn sour, the same people that you give advice to will hold you accountable to any mistakes due to your tip. They want the results without knowing the hard work it takes to reach there.
